# 📖 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive financial and valuation analysis of **Microsoft Corporation (NASDAQ: MSFT)** using publicly available financial statements and market valuation metrics.

Rather than focusing solely on descriptive statistics, the project follows the workflow commonly used by financial analysts and investment professionals to evaluate a company's financial strength, profitability, liquidity, cash flow generation, and market valuation.

The objective is to answer one practical investment question:

> **Does Microsoft's financial performance justify its current market valuation?**

The analysis combines historical financial statement analysis with valuation metrics to provide a structured assessment of Microsoft's business quality and long-term investment attractiveness.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to evaluate Microsoft's financial condition from an investor's perspective.

The analysis focuses on answering several key questions:

- Is Microsoft consistently growing its business?
- How profitable is the company?
- Does Microsoft generate sufficient operating and free cash flow?
- Is the balance sheet financially healthy?
- Does the company maintain strong liquidity?
- Does Microsoft carry excessive debt?
- Does the current market valuation appear justified by its financial performance?
